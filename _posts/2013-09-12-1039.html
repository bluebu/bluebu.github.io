Rails4下启动Guard + Spork时候会报错        
ERROR - Guard::Spork failed to achieve its <start>, exception was:
NoMethodError: private method `raise' called for nil:NilClass
临时解决办法:
gem 'spork-rails', github: 'sporkrb/spork-rails'
